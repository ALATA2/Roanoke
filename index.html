ROANOKE

THE 101

Exactly 101 English settlers have landed on Roanoke Island. You must balance their tasks to survive, but the wild is dark and full of terrors.

Rules of Survival:

  • ☀️ Day (30s): Assign woodcutters to gather wood and fishers to catch food. Idle colonists stay in the Fort and consume food.
  • 🌙 Night (15s): A thick fog settles. Anyone left outside the Fort has a chance to vanish into the dark.
  • 🚨 Recall: Tap the "RECALL ALL TO FORT" button before nightfall to save your workforce!
  • 💀 Game Over: If population falls to 0, or food reserves hit 0, the colony is lost.
